  1. Welcome to AussieV8 - The largest online all Australian V8 community bringing together all makes models

    If you're here you either own a V8 or have an interest in them whether it be Ford or Holden, Chev or Cleveland, who can blame you? You can join thousands of other V8 enthusiasts from around Australia, New Zealand and the rest of the world discussing all topics on car modifications, maintenance and racing! Its easy to join and only takes about 30 seconds!

    As a registered member you can:

    • Participate in over 20 forums and browse through hundreds of thousands of posts.
    • Buy and sell all sorts of car related parts! Its like visiting a swap meet without leaving your chair.
    • Communicate privately with other enthusiasts.
    • Find out about many upcoming car events around Australia, and join members in cruises, car shows and other events.
    • Post your own photos to our gallery, or view thousands of assorted user-submitted images of their cars, bikes and boats.
    • Access various other hidden features, such as live chat and a whole lot more.

    So what are you waiting for? Its Free and takes just a moment so register for an account today!.
