The Best in Modern Lighting

YLighting is the largest online store dedicated to modern and contemporary lighting and furniture and a leading destination for modern design enthusiasts of the United States and Canada. For over 15 years, YLighting has dedicated itself to being the premier online retailer of high-end designer brands focused on the modern and contemporary aesthetic.

YLighting's collection of over 18,000 modern lights spans from iconic mid-century modern lighting works to innovative contemporary lighting designed for the home of today. No matter if you need vanity lights for your bathroom lighting, pendants for your kitchen lighting, or recessed, track or cable lighting for a customized lighting application, YLighting has the finest quality options on the market for you to choose from for both residential and commercial lighting projects. Choose between over 250 high-end lighting brands including Artemide, Moooi, George Nelson Bubble Lamps, SONNEMAN Lighting, TECH Lighting and WAC Lighting.

YLighting's modern furniture sibling, YLiving was added to YLighting in 2020. Now, YLighting offers 100+ well-known furniture brands and carries over 6000 furniture and decor products for every room of the home - such as living room furniture, bedroom furniture, kitchen and dining tables, and office desks.

Finding inspiration and ideas for how to light your home or project is half of the fun when choosing between lighting options. Find the perfect addition to your home's decor by shopping for lighting fixtures by color, material and finish. For ideas and inspiration, how to articles, top modern lighting guides, interviews with featured designers, browse YLighting's Modern Lighting Ideas center to enrich your lighting knowledge.

Free Shipping
on orders of $75 or more!*

Orders of $75 and more:
You pay no shipping fees (UPS or FedEx ground) to anywhere in the continental United States. Nice!

*There are a few exceptions, though:

  • Special order, oversized and heavy items may need to be shipped by freight, and a freight shipping surcharge may be applied.
  • Also, some manufacturers require that we add a shipping surcharge to their products. When this is the case, you will see the shipping or freight charge listed on the product page.

Orders less than $75:
Orders that do not meet the $75 threshold are charged a $9.99 flat rate shipping fee.

There are a few exceptions, though:

Hawaii, Alaska & Puerto Rico
If you are shipping to Hawaii, Alaska or Puerto Rico, YLighting offers discounted standard shipping by UPS or FedEx standard shipping. The actual shipping charge (which at minimum is $24) will be calculated during checkout and is based on the order total.

Canada
When shipping to Canada, YLighting only uses Expedited and Express Saver which includes the brokerage fees in the shipping charge. This total does not include the Goods and Services Tax (GST) or customs duties which will be due upon receipt of delivery.

NOTE: Canadian orders will require additional lead times for these vendors: Blomus, Fanimation, Kichler, Marset and Minka Group (Minka Aire, Minka-Lavery, Metropolitan and George Kovacs).

You will receive an email with the adjusted estimated ship date within 2 business days of submitting your order.
